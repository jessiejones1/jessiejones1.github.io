xyz
For this task the focus was computer generation. I have chosen the political
slogan “Make America Great Again” coined by President Donald Trump.
I thought it would be interesting and controversial to change the words
“America”, “Great” and the name of the person quoting. The two xyz
websites are different versions of just this. These words are completely
random. Please note these political leaders did not necessarily say any
of these generated quotes.
image nets
In this task we explored the idea of internet archives. I have a fascination
of the mundane and overlooked, for this reason I choose to explore the
idea of “bananas” as an archive. This website allows the use to choose
their own adventure, with each image allowing the opportunity to connect
to another. The images tell a complicated story, but one that needs to be
told of the in's and out's of Banana Production.
