Contrary to popular belief, banana’s do not actually represent the male genitalia. This is what is referred to as the patriarchy.

this archive was created for the sole use as an university assignment, affectionately known as ImageNets. Overseen by Uriah Grey, the premise is around Hunting + Gathering in the Digital Wilderness asking:  
Are we the sum of our clicks and searches? 
Are we the archivists or are we the archived? 
Are we in control here, or are we engaged in a “cruel struggle for survival” in the digital wilderness?

assuming you’re male (patriarchy am I right) the suggestion is you get to choose your own adventure. Limited to a Banana Adventure, choose as to where you start and finish. As you go though the images have helpful comments which are there for your enjoyment and criticism.
